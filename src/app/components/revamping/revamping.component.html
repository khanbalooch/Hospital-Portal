REVAMPING

The Primary and Secondary Healthcare Department, Government of Punjab started Revamping of hospitals to improve the performance of hospitals. The Revamping process was divided into three phases. THQ Hospital Kehror Pacca stands in Phase-II of revamping process. Under the revamping process following new intiatives were taken at THQ Hospital Kehror Pacca



1. New Management Structure

In order to successfully complete the program objectives in the given timeframe established a dedicated Program Management Unit having technical and administrative expertise

According to the above orgnogram following staff is newly recruited at THQ Hospital Kehror Pacca under supervision of Medical Suprintendent and Higher authorities.

2. Outsourcing of Non-Clinical & Ancillary clinical services

The Department has outsourced Non-clinical and ancillary clinical services to leading service providers. The outsourcing contracts are done on the central level
