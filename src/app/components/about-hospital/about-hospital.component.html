Who We Are ?

Tehsil Quarter Hospital Kehror Pacca was Established in 1992 as 40 bedded hospital with Indoor and Emergency wards. Since its establishment, THQ Hospital Kehror Pacca is continuing its tradition of uncompromising quality, dedicated service and overall excellence to become the dominant full-service independent health facility center in Tehsil Kehror Pacca, Lodhran. THQ Hospital's departments and all staff (clinical and non-clinical) is dedicated to provide diagnostic excellence. Our facility utilizes latest state of art instrumentation and medical technology. Our staff consists of Highly qualified Consultants, Surgeons, Medical Officers and Technologists striving to meet the highest standard of care that our clinical practices.

A few strong key features of the Hospital include:

  • Total Occupied Area: 74 Kanal
  • Total Number of Beds: 40 Beds
  • Total Ambulances: 02 Ambulances
  • CCTV Security Cameras installed: 20
  • Clinical Staff: 87
  • Non-Clinical Staff: 26
  • Public Address System: Available

With in the available resources the Hospital and all staff is committed to a formal yet dynamic quality assurance program, Quality Assurance team regularly monitors key quality indicators. Significant variations from established performance thresholds and SDS (Service Delievery Standards) are examined and corrective measures implemented, regularly.
